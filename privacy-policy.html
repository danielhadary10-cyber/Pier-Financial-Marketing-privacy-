Introduction

Pier Financial Advisory ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, advertisements, lead forms, and related services.

By submitting your information through any of our forms or services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Demographic information: age, marital status, number of dependants, and homeownership status
  • Insurance-related information: coverage needs, health status (if voluntarily provided), and current coverage details
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device type, and browsing behavior collected through cookies and similar technologies

How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Facebook and Instagram lead generation forms
  • Our website contact forms
  • Phone conversations with our agents
  • Email correspondence
  • Third-party advertising platforms (Meta/Facebook) that facilitate ad delivery and lead capture

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide you with life insurance quotes from multiple carriers
  • Contact you regarding your quote request or inquiry
  • Match you with appropriate insurance products and carriers
  • Improve our advertising and marketing efforts
  • Measure the performance of our advertising campaigns
  • Comply with applicable state insurance regulations and licensing requirements
  • Communicate important updates about your inquiry or policy

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Insurance carriers: To obtain quotes and process applications on your behalf. As an independent agency, we work with 20+ carriers to find the best fit for your needs.
  • Service providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our business, such as CRM platforms, email services, and advertising platforms (including Meta/Facebook).
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

Facebook and Meta Technologies

We use Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising tools to reach potential customers. This includes:

  • Lead generation ads: When you submit a form through our Facebook or Instagram ads, your information is transmitted to us through Meta's platform.
  • Conversion tracking: We may use Meta's Conversions API and/or Facebook Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. This may involve sharing limited data (such as email or phone number in hashed form) with Meta to attribute conversions.
  • Custom audiences: We may use hashed customer data to create custom audiences for advertising purposes on Meta's platforms.

For more information on Meta's data practices, please visit Meta's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you request deletion of your information, we will process your request in accordance with applicable law.

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
